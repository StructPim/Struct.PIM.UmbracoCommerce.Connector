StructPIM Settings

Welcome to the StructPIM settings area. From this section you can setup and manage your StructPIM connection to Umbraco Commerce.

Setup

Start by going through below links to setup StructPIM:

  • Setup connection to StructPIM through Setup
  • Configure how PIM data should map to Umbraco Commerce entities in Data models
  • Localize each individual Stores and make prices and inventory available
  • Go to settings > Examine Management Examine Management and rebuild the CategoryIndex and ProductIndex
Important!

To keep CategoryIndex and ProductIndex in sync with PIM you can setup below webhooks:

  • ProductUpdate > [YourUmbracoCommerceDomain]/umbraco/api/pimwebhooks/HandleProductUpdate (default data mode)
  • VariantUpdate > [YourUmbracoCommerceDomain]/umbraco/api/pimwebhooks/HandleVariantUpdate (default data mode)
  • CategoryUpdate > [YourUmbracoCommerceDomain]/umbraco/api/pimwebhooks/HandleCategoryUpdate (default data mode)
  • GlobalListValueUpdate > [YourUmbracoCommerceDomain]/umbraco/api/pimwebhooks/HandleGlobalListUpdate
